How does this work!?

  • Choose a sound from the drop down menu for each instrument
  • Click on any circle to activate that pad
  • Choose as many pads in each track as you want
  • Press play; then each activated pad will play its sound in order
  • Use the mute buttons to mute an entire track
  • Use the tempo slider to adjust the speed
